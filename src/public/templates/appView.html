Open Philosophybeta

Welcome to the beta release of Open Philosophy, an open access collection of public domain philosophy texts - from Heraclitus to Hegel, Schopenhauer to Schlegel.

If you’d like to start reading, adding, curating and annotating texts you can sign up for an account.

Open Philosophy is powered by the open source TEXTUS platform, developed by the Open Knowledge Foundation. If you’re interested in contributing to the project, we’d love to hear from you.
