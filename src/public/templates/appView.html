Welcome to Open Philosophy

Open Philosophy enables you to freely and easily share, annotate and compile public domain philosophy texts.

It is an entry point into the vast amount of digitised public material now available on the web allowing scholars, students and enthusiasts to collaborate around some of the greatest and most profound works in human history.

Open Philosophy is a community driven project. All content is uploaded and maintained by groups of volunteers. Register to use Open Philosophy right away.

The site is based on software from the TEXTUS project, which is an open source platform for the humanities currently in the BETA stage of development. Discussion about improvements to the platform and use cases occur on the Open Humanities Discussion List - join the conversation today.

If you are a coder would like to get involved in the effort to develop TEXTUS further, please join the Open Humanities Developer mailing list.

If you notice issues with this site, please report them to: info@textusproject.org or on GitHub.

All content on this site is in the public domain and is therefore free to use, re-use and re-distribute. If you identify material on this site that is in copyright please email info@textusproject.org.
